Welcome to Icon Universal Poly

I-Con Universal Polytechnic, Osogbo is a private institution. It was established in November, 2015. The institution, which is situated at KM 4, Gbongan-Ibadan Road, Osogbo, the capital of Osun State, was founded by a United State of America-based business tycoon and journalist of international repute, Mr. Ibrahim Adeoti. The Polytechnic is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities. At present, it operates from its temporary site at KM 4, Gbongan-Ibadan Road, Opposite Sterling Bank, Ogo Oluwa Area, Osogbo. However, the Polytechnic has acquired a 50-acres of land at Oke-Osun, near Fountain University, Osogbo as its permanent site.

The Polytechnic has been approved by the National Board for Technical Education (NBTE) with eight (8) courses at present which it looks forward to increasing forthwith. Besides, the institution, after being approved by the NBTE has also been recognized by the Joint Admission and Matriculation Board (JAMB).
