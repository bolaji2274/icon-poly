Who We Are

I-Con Universal Polytechnic, Osogbo is a private institution. It was established in November, 2015. The institution, which is situated at KM 4, Gbongan-Ibadan Road, Osogbo, the capital of Osun State, was founded by a United State of America-based business tycoon and journalist of international repute, Mr. Ibrahim Adeoti. The Polytechnic is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities. At present, it operates from its temporary site at KM 4, Gbongan-Ibadan Road, Opposite Sterling Bank, Ogo Oluwa Area, Osogbo. However, the Polytechnic has acquired a 50-acres of land at Oke-Osun, near Fountain University, Osogbo as its permanent site. The Polytechnic has been approved by the National Board for Technical Education (NBTE) with eight (8) courses at present which it looks forward to increasing forthwith. Besides, the institution, after being approved by the NBTE has also been recognized by the Joint Admission and Matriculation Board (JAMB).

Our MIssion Vission

Vision Statement The vision of I-Con Universal Polytechnic is to be a leader among world-class private polytechnics by advancing knowledge through high quality and relevant education for our students, by: i. encouraging and fostering entrepreneurship, ii. conducting leading edge research and scholarship, specifically in engineering, sciences, environmental studies and management studies, and iii. promoting an intellectual environment that is anchored on the tenets of open dialogue and enquiry and a deep and abiding appreciation for the entire spectrum of human conditions. Our Core Values In pursuance of the above, the Polytechnic will be guided by the following core values: i. Excellence in academic and moral standards ii. Efficiency and diligence in service delivery
